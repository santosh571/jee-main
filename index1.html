    JEE Main 2024 Eligibility Criteria

    Age Limit

    There is no fixed age limit.

    Educational Qualification

    Candidates should have cleared the 10+ 2 or equivalent from a recognized board . Candidates who have cleared the 12th board exam in 2022 and 2023 or will appear for the exam in 2024 are also eligible.

    Subjects in the Qualifying Exam

    The candidates should have cleared the following subjects-

  • Language
  • Physics
  • Mathematics
  • Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Technical vocational subject
  • Any other subject

    • Admission Criteria for NITs, IITs, and CFTIs

  • For B.Tech/ B.Arch/ B.Plan :

    • Minimum 75% marks in 12 Board Examination for GENERAL and OBC

    Minimum 65% marks in 12 Board Examination for SC/ST Category

    or Should have in top 20 percentile in respective Board

    For B.Arch in institutes other than NITs, IIITs, and CFTIs:

    50 per cent aggregate marks in Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics in the 10+ 2 or equivalent examinations.
